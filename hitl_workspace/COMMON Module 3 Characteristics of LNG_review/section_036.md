## Pool Fire

If immediate ignition occurs, a pool fire will result.  The extent of the pool spreading (diameter), and flame height will depend on several factors including the flow rate of LNG, the spill surface type (water or land), the spill surface geometry, spill surface roughness, release composition, release temperature, ambient wind speed, ambient temperature, ambient relative humidity, and containment pit or bounded area.
