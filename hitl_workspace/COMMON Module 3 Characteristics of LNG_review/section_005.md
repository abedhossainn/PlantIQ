## Learning Objectives for this Self-Study Module

1. State the chemical compounds of hydrocarbons.
2. Describe the physical properties of hydrocarbons.
3. Name the three states of matter.
4. Describe the properties of gases.
5. Describe the key definitions of the characteristics of LNG.
6. Discuss the fire hazards of LNG.
7. Describe the types of LNG hazards.
