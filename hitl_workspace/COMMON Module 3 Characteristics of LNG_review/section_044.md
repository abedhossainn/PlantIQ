## PPE

The adverse effect of a cryogenic liquid upon the skin can be minimized by wearing the proper personal protective equipment (PPE).  These items include, but are not limited to:

- Rain slickers
- Face shields and goggles
- Hard hat
- Rubber coated gloves
- Rubber boots
