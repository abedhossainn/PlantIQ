## INTRODUCTION

This self-study module is an introduction to the characteristics of LNG. Understanding the overall function and characteristics of LNG provides you with the information required to work with LNG safely and economically.

In this module, we review the following main topics:

- Hydrocarbons
- States of Matter
- Properties of Gases
- Characteristics of LNG
- Physical Properties of Hydrocarbons
- Hazards of LNG
- Understanding LNG Fire Hazards
- Types of LNG Hazards
