Learning Objective 7

The potential hazards of most concern to LNG facility personnel and the surrounding community flow from the basic properties of natural gas.

Describe the types of LNG hazards.

Primary containment (tanks, piping, etc.), secondary containment (dikes, walls, spillways, etc.), safeguard systems (ESD, fire and gas detection, etc.), and separation distance provide multiple layers of protection.  These measures provide protection against hazards associated with LNG.
