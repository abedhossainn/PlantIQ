## Boyle's Law

Boyle's Law states that, for any isothermal (same temperature) process, the volume of a fixed mass of gas is inversely proportional to its absolute pressure ' (refer to Figure 5 ).

<!-- formula-not-decoded -->

Remember that all units should be 'absolute' and that:

Absolute pressure = Gauge pressure + Atmospheric pressure

Simply stated, Boyle's Law states that: At constant temperature, if the volume of a gas is reduced by half, its pressure is doubled.
