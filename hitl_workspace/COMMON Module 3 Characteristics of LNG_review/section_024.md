## Charles' Law

Charles Law states that at a Constant Pressure , if the absolute temperature is doubled, then its volume will also be doubled.

For any isobaric (same pressure) process, the volume of a given mass of gas is directly proportional to its absolute temperature (refer to Figure 6 ).
