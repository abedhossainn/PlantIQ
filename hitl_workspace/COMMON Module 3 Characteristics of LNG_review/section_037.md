## NOTE

A pool fire is a type of diffusion flame where a layer of volatile liquid fuel is evaporating and burning.

If the liquid pool is unconfined and the inventory of LNG is large, the fire will continue to burn until all the fuel is exhausted by the pool fire.  It is not practical or even possible to extinguish large LNG pool fires resulting from large spills of LNG unless the flow of LNG feeding the pool can be stopped.
