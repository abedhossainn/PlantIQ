## Emergency Action Steps

The following steps should be taken immediately to treat cryogenic burns:

1. Immediately flush the affected areas with clean, cool water.
2. Wrap the affected areas with loose, clean bandages.
3. DO NOT rub the affected areas.
4. Seek immediate medical assistance .
