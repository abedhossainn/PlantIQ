## Module Layout

This self-study module provides the basis for training in your specific job responsibilities as they relate to LNG.  To safely complete all assigned tasks, you must have an in-depth knowledge of how the process works and operates.

Each lesson in this module builds on the foundation established in the previous lesson. These lessons include a general description including the gas flow path, major equipment, and system operation and control.

Major equipment components are listed in this document with their proper name and Tag Number, i.e., Feed Gas Heater (5E101). Tag numbers are used to standardize identification of those components in drawings, process flow diagrams, P&amp;IDs (Piping and Instrumentation Diagrams) (Piping and Instrumentation Diagrams) and on the actual equipment in the field.

Notes, Cautions, Warnings, and Environmental information may be listed throughout each lesson. This is critical information related to operations, personnel safety, and environmental protection.
