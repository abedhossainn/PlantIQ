## Learning Objective 1

State the chemical compounds of hydrocarbons.

There are millions of different ways in which carbon and hydrogen atoms can be connected to form hydrocarbon molecules.  To make it easier to classify them, these materials are grouped into several large families, e.g., the ' paraffin ' family .'

The smallest hydrocarbon molecule is Methane , which is the major constituent of natural gas.  It has one carbon atom connected to four hydrogen atoms.  Its chemical formula is represented as CH4 and its molecular structure is shown below in Figure 1 .
